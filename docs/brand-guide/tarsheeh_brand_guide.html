Brand Essence

Mission

We strive to make professional recruitment accessible to businesses that cannot afford a full HR team, and effortless for those that already have one.

Personality

  • Precise, not clinical
  • Confident, not arrogant
  • Intelligent, not overwhelming
  • Premium, not exclusionary
  • Efficient, not rushed

Brand Voice

  • Declarative and direct
  • Minimal punctuation flourish
  • Never uses jargon
  • Serif tone in headlines
  • Calm authority in body copy
Brand Positioning Statement

Recruitment should not require a dedicated team to do well. Tarsheeh.cv is a multi-agent AI platform that makes the entire process Something to businesses can run without the overhead of a full HR operation team, and effortless for those that already have one. It reads every CV, evaluates every candidate against your job profile, and returns a ranked shortlist with full reasoning in minutes, not days.

Secondary Text / Labels

Gold: When to Use

  • Logo and wordmark accent (.cv)
  • Decorative rules, thin dividers, corner marks
  • Primary button fill
  • Active border on focused inputs
  • Section number labels
  • Score bars and progress indicators

Gold: When Not to Use

  • Large filled background areas
  • Body paragraph text
  • Error or warning states (use red/amber)
  • More than one element per screen without breathing room
  • Borders wider than 1px unless primary CTA

Logo Rules
  • FontCormorant Garamond, font-weight 300 for "TARSHEEH", font-weight 400 for ".CV"
  • Letter-spacing0.2em on the full wordmark. Never reduce below 0.15em.
  • Colour — Dark"TARSHEEH" in Gold Pale (#EDD38A), ".CV" in Gold (#B8922E)
  • Colour — Light"TARSHEEH" in #4A3A1A (dark warm brown), ".CV" in Gold (#B8922E)
  • Clear SpaceMinimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "T" on all sides
  • Minimum Size16px font-size minimum for digital. Never scale below legibility of the full stop.
  • Never DoDo not rotate, stretch, recolour to non-brand colours, apply glow or shadow effects, or separate "TARSHEEH" from ".CV" onto different lines.
  • Corner MarkThe bracket corner motif (18–28px, 1px gold line) may accompany the logo on hero sections and cover pages. Never use it as a standalone logo replacement.
